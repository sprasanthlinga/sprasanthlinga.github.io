IP Address and Subnet Mask define the Subnet For Example IP address 172.31.1.0 and Subnet Mask of 255.255.240.0 means that the subnet address ranges from 172.31.0.0 to 172.31.15.255 Another notation is 172.31.1.0/28

The first Address is the Network Address and the last Address is the Broadcast Address.

They are reserved and cannot be assigned to any node.
